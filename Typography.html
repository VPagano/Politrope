Kinetic Typography

The project was developed during the Audio Visual Studio at Politecnico di Milano to acquire motion graphic design skills. Each person designs a kinetic typographic animation of an extract of the Italo Calvino audiobook "Il Visconte Dimezzato". The chosen quote tells about the nobleman's nephew's arrival in Pratofungo, the city of lepers. He is searching for his wet nurse Sebastiana.

NARRATIVE STRUCTURE

The challenge was to tell the chosen quote only through typography. Initially, typographic animation focuses on animating keywords and trying to render their meaning visually. As the video continues, the animation's protagonist becomes the entire text, animated by following the narrator's words. The choice to make a climax animation stems from wanting to make a video that follows both the meaning of the words and the intonation of the narrator's voice. Moreover, cause the quote is set on a sunny morning, yellow is the primary colour of the animation.
