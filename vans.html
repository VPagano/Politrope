Spring off the wall:
New designers drop in

A selected group of young creative designers designed the Vans exhibition at Milano Design week 2018. After one week of a multidisciplinary Vans' workshop held at Politecnico di Milano, the EMEA Vans team selected the best project idea. The exhibition aimed to create a physical experience that would bring young people closer to the pillars of the Vans.

DROP IN INTO VANS WORLD

The core of the whole experience is the drop-in. For drop-in, we refer to the feeling of emptiness you get when you try to perform the drop-in trick. The drop-in is one of the very first tricks one learns when starting to skate. A jump into "the Vans world" reproduces the drop-in sensation.

FEEL SKATEBOARD SENSATIONS

Vans started its history as a skate shoe brand. Its values revolve around skate and its imagery. The concept of the experience revolves around the three primary sensations of skateboarding: vertigo, balance and speed. Three rooms physical translate the primary sensations.

VISUAL COMMUNICATION

The communication of the entire installation revolves around the three symbols of the experience: speed, vertigo and balance and the main colours and fonts of the vans brand. To promote the exhibition, we created a digital campaign on various sites linked to design week, a guerrilla marketing campaign, using stickers scattered around the city of Milan and flyers distributed to multiple organisations.
